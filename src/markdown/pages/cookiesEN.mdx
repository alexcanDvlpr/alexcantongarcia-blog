## WHAT IS A COOKIE?

A cookie is a file that is downloaded to your computer when you access certain web pages or blogs.

Cookies allow a website to, among other things, store and retrieve information about your browsing habits or your device, and depending on the information they contain and how you use your device, they can be used to recognize you.

The user's browser stores cookies on the hard drive only during the current session, using minimal memory space and not harming the computer. Cookies do not contain any specific personal information, and most are deleted from the hard drive at the end of the browser session (known as session cookies).

Most browsers accept cookies as standard and, regardless of them, allow or prevent temporary or stored cookies through security settings.

Cookies are associated with the browser, not the person, so they usually do not store sensitive information such as credit card or banking details, photos, or personal information. The data stored is technical, statistical, personal preferences, content customization, etc.

## TYPES OF COOKIES

To offer you a better user experience, gather analytical data, store and retrieve information about your browsing habits or your device, and support our activity, this website uses both first-party and third-party cookies.

### What types of cookies does this website use?

- **Technical cookies:** These allow the user to browse the website, platform, or app and use different options or services such as controlling data traffic, identifying sessions, accessing restricted areas, remembering items in a cart, completing purchases, signing up for events, using security features, storing multimedia content, or sharing content on social networks.

- **Personalization cookies:** These allow the user to access the service with predefined general features such as language, browser type, or regional settings.

- **Analytics cookies:** These, either handled by us or third parties, allow us to quantify the number of users and perform statistical measurement and analysis of the usage of our services. Your navigation is analyzed to improve the offer of products or services.

- **Advertising cookies:** These, either handled by us or third parties, allow us to manage the advertising spaces on the website as effectively as possible, tailoring the ad content to the requested service or your usage of our website.

- **Behavioral advertising cookies:** These enable the most effective management of advertising spaces that the publisher may include on a website, application, or platform. These cookies store information on user behavior through continuous observation of their browsing habits, which allows a specific profile to be developed for targeted advertising.

- **Third-party cookies:** This website may use third-party services, such as those provided by Google, to collect information for statistical purposes, user behavior analysis, and other services related to the website and internet use.
